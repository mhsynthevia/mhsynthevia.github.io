Welcome to MH Synthevia

MH Synthevia is a dynamic and fast-growing Information Technology company headquartered in Oud Metha, Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Founded in 2025, we have rapidly established ourselves as a trusted technology partner for businesses across the UAE and the wider Middle East region. Our office is located at Office Court Building 228, Oud Maitha Road, with PO Box 115304.

With a strong team of 51-200 skilled professionals, we specialize in delivering end-to-end IT solutions that help organizations embrace digital transformation, improve operational efficiency, and stay secure in an increasingly complex technological landscape. We combine global best practices with deep understanding of the local business environment to deliver solutions that truly matter.

Our core expertise lies in five key areas: Custom Software Development, Cloud Solutions (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), Cybersecurity, Strategic IT Consulting, and Advanced Data Analytics & Business Intelligence. Whether you need a bespoke enterprise application, secure cloud migration, comprehensive cybersecurity protection, or actionable insights from your data — MH Synthevia has the expertise and experience to deliver exceptional results.
